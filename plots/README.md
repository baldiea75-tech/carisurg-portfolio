## Plots

The `plots/` folder contains all visualisations produced across the project, organised below by the week they were created in.

### Week 0: Initial Clinical Triage EDA

- `gender_distribution.png`: distribution of patient gender across the triage dataset
- `gcs_histogram.png`: histogram of Glasgow Coma Scale scores recorded at triage
- `pulse_histogram.png`: histogram of patient pulse readings
- `temp_histogram.png`: histogram of patient temperature readings
- `rr_histogram.png`: histogram of patient respiratory rate readings
- `sbp_vs_dbp.png`: scatter plot comparing systolic and diastolic blood pressure
- `age_vs_pulse.png`: scatter plot comparing patient age and pulse
- `vitals_boxplots.png`: box plots comparing the spread of all vital signs
- `pulse_analytics.png`: summary analytics chart for pulse readings
- `my_plot.png`: exploratory scratch plot from early analysis
- `triage_workflow_diagram.png`: diagram of the end to end triage data workflow

### Week 5: Yale EMMLC Feasibility Assessment

- `missingness_heatmap.png`: heatmap of missing values across all structured columns, confirming zero missingness in the dataset
- `plot1_esi_distribution.png`: distribution of patients across the five ESI triage levels
- `plot2_demographics.png`: patient race and insurance status distribution
- `plot3_vitals_distributions.png`: distribution of all seven triage vital signs after cleaning
- `plot4_chief_complaints.png`: the fifteen most common presenting complaints at triage
- `plot5_correlation_heatmap.png`: correlation matrix between vitals, age and ESI
- `plot6_esi_by_arrivalmode.png`: mean ESI level by how the patient arrived at the ED
- `plot7_esi_by_insurance.png`: mean ESI level by patient insurance status
- `plot8_age_by_esi.png`: age distribution across each ESI level
- `plot9_complaints_by_age.png`: chief complaints most associated with older or younger patients
- `plot10_urgent_complaints.png`: chief complaints with the highest and lowest average acuity
- `plot11_esi_by_race.png`: mean ESI level by patient race
